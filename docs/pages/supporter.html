Young People Playing at the Beach

April 2023

Early Supporters Series

  • A series of 50 unique Cubism style images of young people playing at the beach by DALL-E.

    Each image can be purchased as an NFT by anyone that wishes to support the selfdriven project @ USD16 each.

  • Early supporters with NFTs from this series are allocated 40 selfdriven Foundation (SDF) tokens for each image purchased.

    The SDF tokens will be sent to the wallet (i.e. address) used to purchase the NFT.

  • NFTs are of type "Early Supporter", so any wallets containing this NFT will qualify for any future "Early Supporter" bonuses!

The images will be delivered as a NFTs using IPFS and the Cardano blockchain.

Each image will have a unique ID and be minted as a unique ARTi (artomics ID) token - allowing it to be traded.

Standard royality fee based on trading is 20%.

Funds from purchasing and subsequent trading (and associated royality) of the Art (as ARTI NFTs) will be allocated as follows:

10% to creating artist.

10% to the selfdriven Treasury.

80% towards project funding as managed by the foundation.
