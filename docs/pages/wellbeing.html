Wellbeing

Learning communities as a safe place to learn and grow.

Learner Growth & Support

The selfdriven Operating System works to positively impact learner & learning-partner wellbeing.

Indirect

Self-awareness via values in the Supported Self-Driven Learning Framework.

Visible and shareable recognition of growth and positive behaviour as endorsements, achievements and reflections by learning-partners.

Making it authentic and fun via play based experiences.

Direct

In app "How Are You Going?" capturing of learner anxiety levels and source of anxiety (if anxiety exists) and other wellbeing converns they have.

Connections to professional wellbeing specialists.

Summarised dashboard for professional learning facilitators to build strategies around community level anxiety or sources of anxiety.
