Shareable Summary
The Selfdriven Foundation and Cloud Services are two interdependent organisations with their own purposes, structures, and governance mechanisms.
The Selfdriven Foundation functions as an autonomous organisation, with its governance and direction being determined by the SDF token holders. The Foundation focuses on research and engagement with an open-source (CCO) protocol framework, aiming to create a less harmful society. It also supports education about the value and technology of blockchains, specifically Cardano, with the goal of creating decentralised societies and promoting self-sovereign identity.
Selfdriven Cloud Services operates as a registered Australian business (Selfdriven Pty Ltd). The team covers various areas such as direction, engagement, lab, and operations. This entity runs the services to support common apps and APIs and has agreements with learning communities to provide these resources. It can also provide professional support services as required.
Both organisations manage the keys for token generation on the Cardano blockchain. The Foundation's SDF tokens are based on human obligations and effort. The Cloud Services' tokens, including SDI (SDV), SDA, SDAX, SDD, SDL & SDC, are based on the activity data managed within the Selfdriven Cloud Service
