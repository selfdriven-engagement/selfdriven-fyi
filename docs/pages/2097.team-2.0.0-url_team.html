Shareable Summary
The selfdriven Foundation team is a collective of people who are passionate about education and its potential to create a least harmful society. The team consists of various types of members including Founding Members, Advisory Members, and Community Members. Each category of members has distinct roles and responsibilities.
Key members of the team include:
  • Mark Byers, a Founding Member with over 25 years of experience in internet-based technologies and co-founder of entityOS.
  • Corinne Phillips, also a Founding Member and Research Facilitator, who has a background in environmental and health sciences and a passion for improving societies through technology.
  • Maria Florencia Amigó, an Advisor specializing in Learning Frameworks & Experience-Based Learning.
  • Mark Harricks, an Advisor focusing on Product & Marketing.
  • Nic Fulton, another Advisor who specializes in Internet & Data​.
In addition, there is a Decentralised Team and a community of people who contribute their efforts to the design, build, and support of selfdriven​.
The responsibilities of team members vary, and they are recognized for their efforts through the allocation of SDF tokens. Founding members typically focus about 70% of their energy on their primary area, but this may be distributed across multiple focus areas as the team continues to grow. The team is in a constant state of growth, especially during the startup phase, and they are open to collaboration and new additions to the foundation.
