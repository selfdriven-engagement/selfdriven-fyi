### Building Trust  
Helping learners and learning-partners build trust in learning activity, enabling more efficient collaboration.

### Enabling Self-Driven Learning  
Allow learners to self-drive their learning in a managed system.  
Learning-partners can then focus on learners that need the most support at any moment in time.

### Recognising Experience  
Recognition of previous formal and informal learning.  
We learn when we do.

### Next Steps  
Using the simple next steps app to record and share learning experience between learners, learning-partners (individuals or organisations).
