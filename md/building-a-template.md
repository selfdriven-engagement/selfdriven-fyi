#### Template Components

| Component | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Milestones** | Determine what milestones exist in the project. <br> [Template Worksheet](/assets/img/selfdriven-templates-structure-worksheet-light.pdf) • [Milestones Worksheet](/assets/img/selfdriven-templates-structure-milestones-worksheet-light.pdf) |
| **Duration** | Elapsed duration of the project (days from start to end). |
| **Sharing** | Identify any source information and its sharing/licensing requirements. |
| **Support Items** | Items that will help the learner meet the objectives. |
| **Resources** | General notes and links about the project. |
| **Tasks** | The tasks that need to be completed (measures of accountability). |
| **Collaboration** | How will learners collaborate — individually or in teams? |

## Improvement Cycle

| Phase | Who / Why / What / How | Notes | In template |
|-------|-------------------------|--------|-------------|
| **Listen & Observe** | Who & Why | Collaboration & Communication | `listen` |
| **Interpret** | Why → What | Creativity | `interpret` |
| **Implement** | What → How | Collaboration & Communication, Playing | `implement` |
| **Review** | Reflect on Why, What, How | Critical Reflection & Communication | `review` |
