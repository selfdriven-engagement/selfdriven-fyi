# Apps

_For Learners, Learning-Partners & Community Facilitators_

![selfdriven apps](/assets/img/selfdriven-2-apps-dark.png)

The following apps are built, supported and hosted as cloud services by selfdriven.

All the apps are compatible, serving as specific interfaces to the user's or community's data.

All the app user interface code and data schemas are open source, so you can also build and host your own apps.

---

## You

_All about identity, wellbeing & growth._

Uses the [Growth-Cycle](/growth-cycle) framework.

- **General release:** Q3 2023  
- **Pricing:** Free for learners  

**Features**

- **Growth Profile**  
  Set up challenges (projects) for growth.
- **Flags**  
  Raise flags based on how you are feeling.
- **Agency**  
  Self-drive your learning for agency.
- **Tools**  
  Get tools to help control your life at all levels; your mind & your information.
- **Self-Sovereign Identity**  
  Get a DID.

**App URL:** [you.selfdriven.app](https://you.selfdriven.app)

## Level Up

A simple introductory app to allow learners to record and level up their skills via learning projects and upload verified achievements.

Includes learning reflection (verification) by self and trusted learning-partners.

- **General release:** Q3 2023  
- **Pricing:** Free for learners  

**Features**

- **Skills**  
  The skills to be levelled up.
- **What's Up**  
  Interactions between learners and their learning-partners.
- **To Do Tasks**  
  The tasks relating to levelling up their skills.
- **Connections**  
  Connections to learning-partners.
- **Achievements**  
  The achievements relating to the growth of skills.
- **Reflections**  
  Learner reflections.

**App URL:** [levelup.selfdriven.app](https://levelup.selfdriven.app)  
**Getting Started Guide:**  
- [Level Up getting started guide](https://selfdriven.education/getting-started-app-level-up-learner)  
**More about Level Up:**  
- [/levelup](/levelup)

## Everything

_The core app with everything included._

- **General release:** Q2 2023  
- **Pricing for learning-partners:** Free  
- **Pricing for learners:** Fee per learner (see pricing page)  

[Pricing](https://www.selfdriven.cloud/pricing)

**Features**

- **Capture all Learning Activity**  
  Capture all learning activity, including experience-based learning.
- **More Relevant Measurements of Growth**  
  Measure what is best aligned with the learner, i.e. growth and specific skills based on next steps.
- **Build a Trusted Source for Learning-Partners**  
  Create a trusted source of information for learners and their learning-partners.
- **Reward Contributions / Caring**  
  Use tokens to recognise contributions to self-learning and community.
- **Improvement Cycle**  
  Observe, analyse, implement, review and reflect.
- **Project Based Learning**  
  Manage projects and tasks in conjunction with the improvement cycle.
- **Visualise Growth**  
  Visual representation of learner growth based on core attributes (e.g. focus, grit).
- **Capture Reflections**  
  Capture reflections by self and learning-partners.
- **Check-Ins**  
  Periodically create a check-in and review with learning-partners.
- **Well-being**  
  Directly and indirectly capture information to measure well-being.
- **Achievements / Skills**  
  Capture achievements that can be linked to known skills and shared on the blockchain.
- **Next Steps**  
  Capture plans for next learning.

**App URL:** [selfdriven.app](https://selfdriven.app)
